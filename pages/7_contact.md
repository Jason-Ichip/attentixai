## Section 3: Frequently Asked Questions

**Headline:** "Quick Answers"

### Sales & Product Questions

**Q: What's the difference between Starter, Scale, and Enterprise plans?**
A: Primarily data volume and request throughput. All plans include the same features. See [pricing page](/get-started#pricing).

**Q: Do you offer discounts for annual payment?**
A: Yes—15% discount for annual prepay. Contact sales@attentix.ai for details.

**Q: Can I try Attentix before committing?**
A: Absolutely. Start a free 14-day trial here. No credit card required.

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
A: 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied. No questions asked. (Enterprise contracts are negotiable.)

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: What data formats do you support?**
A: CSV, JSON, Parquet, Avro, Excel, Snowflake, BigQuery, PostgreSQL, S3, GCS, Kafka, REST APIs, and more. [Full list →](/get-started#integrations)

**Q: What's your uptime SLA?**
A: 99.9% (Starter/Scale), 99.99% (Enterprise). View our status page: [status.attentix.ai]

**Q: How long does it take to get predictions after uploading data?**
A: For most datasets, predictions are ready in 2-5 minutes. For very large datasets (>100GB), 15-30 minutes.

**Q: Can I deploy Attentix in my own cloud environment?**
A: Yes. Enterprise customers can deploy on AWS, GCP, or Azure VPC. Private cloud also available upon request.

---

### Security & Compliance

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**
A: Yes. AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. [Security page →](/security)

**Q: Are you HIPAA compliant?**
A: Yes, Attentix AI is HIPAA-eligible for healthcare customers. We sign HIPAA Business Associate Agreements (BAA).

**Q: Do you comply with GDPR?**
A: Yes. We support data portability, right to deletion, and consent workflows. EU data is hosted in Frankfurt.

**Q: Are you SOC2 certified?**
A: Yes, SOC2 Type II. Audit report available to Enterprise customers under NDA.

---

### Implementation & Support

**Q: How long does it take to go from POC to production?**
A: 2-4 weeks with our team, or 2-3 days if you're technical and prefer self-service.

**Q: What if I need custom development?**
A: We offer professional services: custom model development ($50-150K), deployment support, and training. Contact sales@attentix.ai.

**Q: What happens if I want to leave?**
A: You can export all your data and model configurations. No lock-in. [Data export policy →]
